SchoolOS

SchoolOS is a next-generation school management system designed to reduce costs, save time, and improve the quality of education through smart automation and modern design.

It replaces outdated manual processes with efficient tools for attendance, grading, student records, scheduling, announcements, and more—freeing up staff to focus on what truly matters: students.

SchoolOS integrates cutting-edge technologies like computer vision for facial recognition-based authentication, and is built with an environmentally sustainable, low-resource structure—ready to run even on minimal hardware.

With SchoolOS, institutions gain a faster, smarter way to operate—cutting costs, increasing accuracy, and delivering a seamless experience for administrators, teachers, and students alike.

AlignOS

AlignOS is a purpose-driven social networking platform built to help you stay productive, focused, and professionally visible. Unlike traditional platforms, your profile on AlignOS is your actual resume—ready to share or get discovered by companies through smart search.

It features a built-in productivity environment with tools to manage your priorities, track your to-dos, and organize your ongoing learning. You can also explore top-tier educational resources from institutions like MIT, curated to help you grow with intention.

One of AlignOS’s signature features is the immersive countdown system—with visual and audio effects that let you *see* and *feel* the passage of time—designed to keep you accountable and mindful as you work or study.

Forget endless scrolling.

Whether you're learning, building, or searching for your next opportunity—AlignOS keeps you aligned with what truly matters.

RestaurantOS (Under Development)

RestaurantOS is a smart ordering system that replaces printed menus with a dynamic QR code menu—giving your customers instant access to your offerings with a single scan.

Menus can be updated in real-time without reprinting anything, saving both money and time. You can add, remove, or modify items instantly, ensuring your customers always see the latest version.

With just a tap, customers can place their orders directly from their phones—streamlining the process, reducing staff workload, and enhancing the overall dining experience.

Cut printing costs. Reduce wait times. Serve smarter.

RestaurantOS helps you modernize your service with a cleaner, faster, and more sustainable approach to dining.
